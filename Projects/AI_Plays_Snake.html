AI Learns To Play Snake

This project is currently underway. keep track of its progress

Teaching a Deep neural network to learn how to play a game of snake is the goal of this project. So far, I have coded an AI framework and have tested an epsilon greedy reinforcement learning technique. This didn't work as well as I had hoped. The AI learned to avoid the walls and would eat the fruit when it was within 3 squares. However most of the time the AI just went around in circles.

My next approach will be using the advantage actor critic method. This will require me to refactor quite a lot of my code in order to achieve this so I am taking a small break to work on other projects.
